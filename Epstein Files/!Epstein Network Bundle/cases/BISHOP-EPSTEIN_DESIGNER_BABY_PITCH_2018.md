## **CASE FILE: BISHOP-EPSTEIN DESIGNER BABY PITCH (2018)**

### **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

Jeffrey Epstein was pitched a "designer baby" project by Bryan Bishop (biohacker/Bitcoin programmer) in July 2018. The pitch involved anonymous cryptocurrency funding, genetic embryo modification, and operational labs in Ukraine and Mississippi. Epstein engaged seriously through September 2018, but withdrew after the He Jiankui scandal (November 26, 2018) exposed global backlash against gene-edited babies. **Epstein never funded the project.** (Confirmed by MIT Technology Review's Antonio Regalado in Feb 2026 statement.)

### **PROJECT SCOPE (AS PROPOSED)**

**Timeline:** 5-year development plan to first transgenic human birth

**Budget:** ~$9.5M (via anonymous cryptocurrency infrastructure designed by Austin Hill)

**Genetic Enhancements:** Muscle growth without exercise, long-life genes, universal blood type (AB+)

**Methodology:** Gene therapy on male volunteer's testicles to create transgenic sperm (non-invasive alternative to direct embryo injection)

**Infrastructure:**
- Ukraine lab: Mouse surgeries/microinjection experiments
- Mississippi: Human sperm modification trials (5% transfection efficiency)
- Overseas clinics: Medical tourism + anonymous customer acquisition
- Tokyo investors: Secondary funding sources

**Technical Foundation:** diyhpl.us wiki (13+ year living document on embryo genetic engineering) + published peer-reviewed literature

---

### **CRITICAL FINDINGS**

1. **Anonymity was intentional.** Austin Hill (cypherpunk pioneer) specifically structured the financial architecture to hide Epstein's identity. The July 20 crypto meeting preceded the July 23 pitch — this was not ad-hoc.

2. **Epstein was seriously engaged.** He reviewed budgets, technical documents, and peer-reviewed literature. His July 30 comment ("implant embryo, wait 9 months, great ending") shows he was visualizing the outcome, not passing time.

3. **Epstein had legitimate due diligence concerns.** By October 17, he doubted the partners (Simon Black, Max Berry, the Mississippi amateur). This wasn't ideological rejection — it was risk assessment.

4. **He Jiankui scandal was the kill shot.** The global backlash against the Chinese gene-edited babies (November 26–28) convinced Epstein the reputational + legal risk exceeded the benefit. His "please note" email wasn't casual — it was a warning that the scenario he'd been funding had just played out in public.

5. **No evidence of funding.** Despite 4+ months of negotiation, technical review, and budget planning, the EFTA corpus contains no wire transfers, contracts, or commitment letters. Regalado's confirmation (2026) states Epstein never invested.
