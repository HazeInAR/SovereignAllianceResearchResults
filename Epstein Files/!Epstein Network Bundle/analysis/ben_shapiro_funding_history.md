# Ben Shapiro / Daily Wire Funding History

**Purpose:** Document how Ben Shapiro’s Daily Wire project was launched, who funded it, and the known business/media ties that connect the network back to Israeli-aligned entities (funders, sponsors, infrastructure) so we can test the hypothesis that he is being bankrolled to push a pro-Israel propaganda lane.

## Seed funding (2015 launch)
- **Founders:** Ben Shapiro and Jeremy Boreing (both formerly of the Horowitz-funded TruthRevolt) conceived the Daily Wire in 2015 after identifying a gap for conservative digital news/opinion under the same production playbook they used for TruthRevolt.
- **Seed money:** Billionaire brothers Dan and Farris Wilks (Texas fracking/energy billionaires who also fund PragerU) provided the early capital push. In 2015, Farris Wilks personally gave about **$4.7 million** to help launch the Daily Wire, and the brothers maintain an ownership stake via DailyWire Ventures, LLC; the same Guardian exposés that chronicled their $40 million-plus giving list confirm this funding line.[^1]
- **Implications:** The Wilks’ petroleum/energy wealth and ongoing investments in conservative media show they are the behind-the-scenes network enabling Shapiro’s infrastructure. Their philanthropic web includes Israel-friendly causes, which places the Daily Wire inside the same ecosystem as other pro-Israel media investments (PragerU, Dennis Prager). They are the first known funder pushing Shapiro’s brand into the elite media apparatus.

## Present-day sponsors and Israeli infrastructure links
- **ExpressVPN sponsorship:** Daily Wire programming (including Ben Shapiro’s podcasts and event coverage) is regularly underwritten by ExpressVPN. That VPN service is owned by **Kape Technologies**, a British-Israeli digital security firm whose majority owner is Israeli billionaire **Teddy Sagi** and whose CEO is also Israeli (Ido Erlichman).[^2] In 2021 the acquisition was widely reported, and follow-up coverage highlights ExpressVPN’s links to Israel’s tech/intelligence hubs.[^3]
- **Flow-on effects:** Because ExpressVPN funds the Daily Wire, and Kape/ Sagi remain deeply tied to Israeli state-linked venture capital, this provides a direct commercial route that ties the Daily Wire’s revenue stream to an Israeli corporate ecosystem. It also shows how the network’s public-facing sponsors overlap with geo-strategic partners that Shapiro consistently promotes (Israel, pro-Israel lobbying, weaponized antisemitism narratives).
