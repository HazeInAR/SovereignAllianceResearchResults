# Point of Interest: Manolito Royal & Wendy Martinez (Zorro Ranch)

## Confirmed Facts (sourced from EFTAs/office logs)
- **Roles & timeline:** Manolito Royal and Wendy Martinez ran the equestrian/ranch operations from 2011 through June 2012 (see `DOSSIER_MANOLITO_ROYAL.md` and `DOSSIER_WENDY_MARTINEZ.md`). Wendy Martinez replaced Wendy Nun’s responsibilities and took responsibility for stables, horse care, and facility management.
- **Retaliation documented:** By February 2012, emails (EFTA00708772, EFTA00936845, EFTA00936849) show they were restricted from using the stables, computers, and were monitored while facing staffing conflicts. Security cameras and equipment inventories referenced their names.
- **Forced exit:** Epstein ordered Rich Kahn and aides to investigate their departure (EFTA02167520, EFTA02556148, EFTA01879831). Their personal property (keys, credit cards, cell phones) was locked down (EFTA01991143, EFTA02011265).
- **Departure details:** Wendy Martinez resigned June 18, 2012, while Manolito’s resignation was confirmed June 19, 2012. Their home on-site and vehicles/tools were inventoried (EFTA02011265, EFTA02309279).
- **Temporal match to ABQ tip:** Their complaints about stables access, and the abrupt removal align with the alleged tip to Albuquerque FBI regarding girls being kept in stalls (tip document code referenced privately). This is the same period Wendy and Manolito were leaving the ranch.
- **Aftermath:** Neither has spoken publicly or testified—no further records appear after mid-2012 in the document set. This silence is notable given their inside status.

## Supporting Context
- **Financial stress & responsibilities:** Wendy Martinez requested financial clarification for a $60K/year proposal (EFTA01793515) and had documented hardship (EFTA02021691). Her son AJ worked and lived on the ranch.
- **Security incidents tied to ranch:** June 2020 burglary attempt involved Amber Chavez and Cynthia Cano. Detective files mention missing 33 guns from Larry Visoski’s home; photos removed from walls (see `ZORRO_BREAK_IN_CRIME_ANALYSIS.md` and `POTENTIAL_CRIMES_REFERENCE_LIST.md`).
