# Point of Interest: Manolito Royal & Wendy Martinez (Zorro Ranch)

## Confirmed Facts (sourced from EFTAs/office logs)
> **Structure anchors:** R06/R07 cover the stable/AG office inventories cited in February and June 2012. R05–R15 map to the staff dwellings pulsed during the forced exit; R09/R16 map to the garage/utility caches referenced in the Rich Kahn inventory. See `ZORRO_STRUCTURE_CROSSWALK.md` for the full R-number linking.
- **Roles & timeline:** Manolito Royal and Wendy Martinez ran the equestrian/ranch operations from 2011 through June 2012 (see `DOSSIER_MANOLITO_ROYAL.md` and `DOSSIER_WENDY_MARTINEZ.md`). Wendy Martinez replaced Wendy Nun’s responsibilities and took responsibility for stables, horse care, and facility management—**she did not appear to inherit the cloning/medical network that Nun oversaw**. For clarity on the two women, see `A_Tale_of_Two_Wendys.md` to avoid conflating their roles.
- **Retaliation documented:** By February 2012, emails (EFTA00708772, EFTA00936845, EFTA00936849) show they were restricted from using the stables, computers, and were monitored while facing staffing conflicts. Security cameras and equipment inventories referenced their names.
- **Forced exit:** Epstein ordered Rich Kahn and aides to investigate their departure (EFTA02167520, EFTA02556148, EFTA01879831). Their personal property (keys, credit cards, cell phones) was locked down (EFTA01991143, EFTA02011265).
- **Departure details:** Wendy Martinez resigned June 18, 2012, while Manolito’s resignation was confirmed June 19, 2012. Their home on-site and vehicles/tools were inventoried (EFTA02011265, EFTA02309279).
- **Temporal match to ABQ tip:** Their complaints about stables access, and the abrupt removal align with the alleged tip to Albuquerque FBI regarding girls being kept in stalls (tip document code referenced privately). This is the same period Wendy and Manolito were leaving the ranch.
- **Aftermath & Post-exit relocation (NEW):** Neither has spoken publicly or testified—no further records appear after mid-2012 in the document set. This silence is notable given their inside status. However, a critical email (EFTA01913585, 12/19/2012) from Eileen Alexanderson (Black Family Partners, L.P., c/o Apollo Management) reports to Epstein that "Wendy" just boarded a 4.5-hour flight "further south," with Epstein asking whether to consult Brad Okun at PwC. This indicates Wendy Martinez's post-Zorro management was coordinated through **Apollo Global Management (Leon Black's firm)** and involved financial/tax consultation (PwC), suggesting the equestrian operation was part of a larger capital structure managed by Apollo.
