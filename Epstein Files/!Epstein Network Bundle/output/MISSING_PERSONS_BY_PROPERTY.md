## New Mexico (Rancho de San Rafael / Zorro Ranch Area)
1. **West Mesa victims (2001–2006)** – 13 bodies were unearthed near Albuquerque’s West Mesa and at least eight women with the same profile remain missing. The victims discovered in 2009 were aged between 15 and 32 and vanished while living on the margins (sex work, drugs, runaways). Their names + ages: Monica Diana Candelaria (22), Victoria Ann Chavez (26), Syllannia Terene Edwards (15), Doreen Marquez (24), Veronica Romero (28), Jamie Caterina Barela (15), Evelyn Jesus Maria Salazar (27), Virginia Cloven (24), Julie Cyndie Nieto (24), Cinnamon Elks (32), Michelle Gina Valdez (22). These remains mirror the 2003–2006 window in the `ZORRO_TIMELINE_MASTER.md` (see the “girls in stalls” tip around June 2012 and the 2018 cleanups) and align with the same trafficker profile that crossed between the ranch and Albuquerque by road. Investigators have received more than 1,500 tips and the APD is offering a $100,000 reward for any tip leading to an arrest, giving us a direct hook to work backwards from the 2012 “girls in stalls” tip, the nearby Tom Ford/Cerro Pelon rides, and the 2018 security purge. (Sources: “West Mesa murders” Wikipedia entry; KOB.com/KOAT coverage of unsolved missing women; West Mesa Reddit updates; City of Albuquerque reward and anniversary releases.)
2. **Row of still-open missing persons (2001–2006)** – The APD maintains an extended list of the same demographic; the women were aged 19–32 at disappearance. Their names/ages: Darlene Marie Trujillo (20), Martha Jo Lucher (32), Anna Love Vigil (20), Felipa Victoria Gonzales (22), Nina Brenda Herron (21), Jillian Elizabeth Henderson-Ortiz (19), Shawntell Monique Waites (29), Leah Rachelle Peebles (23), Vanessa Reed (24). These victims never turned up after similar disappearances; the APD reward flyers released around the 13th anniversary list 12 names, and the ongoing $100K incentive keeps the cases active. Match their timelines against the 2003–2006 security movements (Bryce/Brice Gordon, Amber/Steve Chavez, the August 2012 purge) documented in the Zorro timeline so we can draw direct parallels. (Sources: same Wikipedia entry citing APD; KOB/KOAT updates; City of Albuquerque release; West Mesa Reddit thread.)
3. **Local rumor: bodies hidden near Zorro** – Reddit posts and New Mexico forums repeated a 2025 email supposedly referencing bodies hidden on the ranch; while unverified, they prompt a towing of missing persons case files against ranch logging/road intervals in Santa Fe County.

The boyfriend (Ryan Bane) left the islands shortly after she was reported missing. Her age/biography fits the type of transient model/crew that the Epstein network recruited. (Sources: BBC news article, Wikipedia entry on her disappearance, LatinAmericanPost, KOHA.net.)
