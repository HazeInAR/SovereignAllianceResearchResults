## Transfer legality
- Florida allows nominal consideration (even $10) on deeds when the parties are related or when the transfer is effectively a gift. The public record simply notes the declared consideration, so the repeated $10 transfers don’t get rejected; they just get recorded. That’s why the 9/11/conspiracy blogs call the timing suspicious, but legally the county records whatever is declared.
