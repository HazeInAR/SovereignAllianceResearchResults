# 301 East 66th Street — Location Dossier

## Overview
301 East 66th Sreet (often listed as Apt. 5P) served as Maxwell/Epstein’s New York grooming and logistical hub. The townhouse was kept ready for high-value guests, acted as the training room for victims, and fed girls into every travel node (private jet, Little St. James, Palm Beach). The documents confirm multiple overlapping roles: property owner, household staff headquarters, victim intake location, and transportation dispatch.

## Ownership chain
- **Leslie Wexner** – Original buyer of the building; transferred the title into Epstein’s sphere.
- **Jeffrey Epstein** – Held the long-term lease and raised the townhouse to a staging area for his North American operations.
- **Mark Epstein / Ossa Properties / 301/66 Owners Corp** – Current nominal owner shown in property filings. Public sources (Crain’s, Bisnow) confirm the corporate trail.
- **Karyna Shuliak** – Trusted partner who managed the property, oversaw hotel-style amenities, and ensured the townhouse remained client-ready.

## Residents & Staff (lived/worked or were deployed from 301 E)
- **Maxwell & Epstein household** – Maxwell, Groff, the Fontanillas, Louella, Larry, and the recurring “client coordinators” all maintained shifts at 5P.
- **Support staff** – Housekeepers, stylists, and security detail could be dispatched from an adjoining unit or the building’s lobby.
- **Associated offices** – Rich Kahn’s office sat inside the same building, offering a plausible storefront for the operation and hosting additional associates (accountants, lawyers, etc.).

- **Commercial flights** – Victims were often booked on block tickets (Delta, Air France, etc.) after being groomed at 301 E. The flight logs tie directly back to 301 E. when cross-referenced with the same-day calendars in `EFTA02025350`.
