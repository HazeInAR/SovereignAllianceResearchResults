# Kushner Family Dossier

## Family overview
- **Charles and Seryl Kushner** immigrated on elite-builder money and built Kushner Companies into a New Jersey/new York real estate platform. Their children — **Jared, Joshua, Nicole, and Dara** — have each been active in the family office, media, and venture spaces; Jared’s marriage to Ivanka Trump folded the family into the Trump orbit by the 2016 campaign.

## Charles Kushner
- Founded Kushner Companies in 1985 and stacked the portfolio with apartment complexes, banks, and commercial assets. In 2005 he pleaded guilty to 18 counts (tax evasion, illegal campaign donations, witness tampering) and served two years at the Federal Prison Camp in Montgomery before being pardoned by the Trump administration on December 23, 2020. In 2025 he was tapped as U.S. ambassador to France and Monaco in the second Trump administration, further deepening the family’s overlap with high-level diplomacy. (Sources: DOJ press release, AP News, Wikipedia)

## Jared Kushner
- Jared Corey Kushner (born 1981) took over the family business from his father, expanded into Manhattan real estate, bought *The New York Observer* (2006), and was announced as a senior advisor in the 2016 campaign. From 2017–2021 he served as Trump’s senior advisor and director of the Office of American Innovation, leading the Abraham Accords, the First Step Act, the USMCA negotiations, and supervising parts of the COVID response. After the administration he founded Affinity Partners, the private-equity firm that raised guest capital from Gulf sovereign funds (notably Saudi Arabia’s PIF) and stayed under Senate scrutiny for possible foreign influence ahead of the 2024 election. In 2025 he reappeared as an informal adviser during the second Trump administration, acting as a diplomatic intermediary on Gaza and Ukraine while pitching new media deals that took him back into Hollywood/tech through partnerships with the Ellison family during the December 2025 Warner Bros. takeover bid. (Sources: Wikipedia, NYTimes, Guardian, BBC, Oversight Democrats)

## Siblings & Ivanka Trump
- **Joshua Kushner** runs Thrive Capital/sibling tech VC and is married to Karlie Kloss. **Nicole** and **Dara Kushner** manage philanthropic and family real estate operations alongside their mother Seryl. Jared’s wife **Ivanka Trump** converted to Modern Orthodox Judaism in 2009, served as an unpaid White House advisor, and remains a key node between the Kushner and Trump dynasties. (Sources: Wikipedia, Town & Country, Business Insider)

## Elite connections & network levers
- The family ties link the Trump White House, Gulf sovereign wealth (MBS/PIF/UAE firms), Israeli normalization deals (Abraham Accords), and the Ellison-backed media-finance wing. Affinity Partners’ July–October 2025 deal pipeline included gulf capital, Larry Ellison’s entertainment sphere, and Israeli venture capital partners such as Elad Gil; the same networks are now flagged inside the elite, Middle East, and gulf context layers so the Kushner node can feed into the broader geopolitical web. (Sources: Forbes, NYTimes, Guardian)
