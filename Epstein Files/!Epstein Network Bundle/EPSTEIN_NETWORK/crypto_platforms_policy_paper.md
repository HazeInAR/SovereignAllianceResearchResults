# Presidential Control of Cryptocurrency Platforms — Key Findings
**Source:** 2025 research paper "Presidential Control of Cryptocurrency Platforms," Nov 27, 2025 (updated edition). Stored copy extracted from `1216a619-ca6d-436b-836d-260e810132cf.pdf` in raw dropbox.

## Highlights & Evidence
1. **World Liberty Financial / USD1 stablecoin**
   - Launch: September 16, 2024 announcement by Donald Trump (Chief Crypto Advocate) and sons; co-founded by Zach Witkoff (CEO), Zachary Folkman, Chase Herro. Steve Witkoff introduced Trump family to the co-founders months earlier.
   - Token structure: WLFI governance token (100B cap, non-transferable until July 2025, founder-heavy tokenomics). USD1 is a fiat-backed stablecoin, backed by short-term Treasuries custodized by providers like BitGo, with 79% of supply on Binance (BNB Chain). Expansion timeline includes Ethereum, BNB, TRON, Aptos, and Solana/Raydium.
   - Revenue: 75% of WLFI net proceeds to DT Marks DeFi LLC, paper holdings ~22.5B WLFI tokens (~$3.4–5B value by Sept 2025), plus ~$108–135M annual treasury yield from USD1 at $2.7B circulation.
   - Income reported: Trump OGE 2025 disclosure records $57.4M from WLFI operations (late 2023-Dec 2024), dwarfing other lines; crypto wealth now ~73% of reported net worth.

2. **Conflict-of-interest pattern**
   - May 1, 2025 MGX (Abu Dhabi sovereign fund) deployed $2B into Binance using USD1 instead of other stablecoins, creating direct foreign capital flows to Trump-controlled asset.
   - Allegations: regulatory pauses for certain foreign investors; stablecoin law crafted to benefit Trump crypto stack; pardon granted to Binance founder after USD1 adoption; sanctions concerns (entities tied to NK, Iran, Russia using governance tokens).
   - Quote: Elizabeth Oyer (DOJ official) calling the sequence “corruption.”

3. **Infrastructure & partnerships**
   - Includes DWF Labs (market maker/lp) $25M investment April 2025 and liquidity commitments, Binance integration, lending/trading fees.
   - Tokenomics shifts (Sept 2024 vs Oct 2024) concentrated control among DT Marks, Axiom Management Group, WC Digital Fi.
   - USD1’s rapid adoption across blockchains indicates global exposure and reliance on centralized exchanges (Binance). 

4. **Methodology & sourcing**
   - Claims cross-referenced against SEC filings, DOJ/Treasury announcements, Congressional testimony, blockchain analysis (Accountable.US, Arkham, Nansen, Chainalysis), major media outlets, legal analyses, Tangible primary docs (SEC Form D Oct 29 2024, WLFI Gold Paper, press releases, court filings).
