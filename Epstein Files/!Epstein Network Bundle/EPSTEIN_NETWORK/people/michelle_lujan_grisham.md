# Michelle Lujan Grisham Dossier — New Mexico Political Finance

## Summary
Michelle Lujan Grisham (Democrat) has been the governor of New Mexico since January 1, 2019, building a statewide machine that now includes five election cycles (House, congressional primaries, and two gubernatorial races) since 2012 and nearly **$30 million** raised, according to her FollowTheMoney profile (eid 14043056). Before the governorship she spent two terms in Congress representing New Mexico’s 1st District, then chaired the Democratic Governors Association and even ran for the Democratic nomination in 2020. Her fundraising base is heavily weighted toward **finance, insurance, real estate, energy, and party infrastructure donors**—the same sectors that regularly appear on the Southern Trust / Deutsche Bank ledgers we already track; keeping her network on the watchlist tells us whether any of those donors later surface in the Jericho, Carbyne, or Delray wires.

- **New Mexico governor (2019–present)** — Oversaw the state during the pandemic, re-elected in 2022, and served as chair/vice-chair of the Democratic Governors Association, which recycled contributions across the elite donor list (see FollowTheMoney and AWPC biography).
