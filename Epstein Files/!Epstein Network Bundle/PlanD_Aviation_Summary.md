# Plan D / Elysium Aviation Summary

**Objective:** document the corporate, financial, and operational links connecting Jeffrey Epsteins Plan D LLC aircraft to Leon Black / Elysium while showing how the jet was kept outside Apollo Global Managements public filings.

## 1. Ownership & Financing
- **Plan D LLC (USVI, founded Oct 19, 2012)**: operating agreement/articles list Jeffrey Epstein as the sole member; Business Basics VI (St. Thomas) serves as the registered agent. *(Docs: EFTAO0308255, EFTAO1583446/49)*
- **Aircraft registration**: FAA records name Plan D LLC as the owner of Gulfstream GV-SP serial 5173, tail number N212JE (previously N415LM), with Larry Visoski managing the account and requesting the tail change in 2018. *(Docs: EFTAO1304318, EFTAO1242519, EFTAO0023439)*
- **Leon Black financing**: Promissory note dated Mar 27, 2017 obligates Plan D to pay $28.5M plus interest to Leon Black c/o Elysium Management LLC (445 Park Ave). AML/compliance records show a $22.5M wire from BV70 LLC on Mar 31, 2017 described as a fee for Epsteins client; Deutsche Bank statement captures that transfer along with follow-on deposits/outflows. *(Docs: EFTAO0592523, EFTAO0603342, EFTAO0140453, EFTAO0140148, EFTAO01286781, EFTAO00646083)*
- **Funds trail**: Exhibit A and Gratitude America transaction logs highlight repeated payments from BV70/Black family entities into Plan D and Epsteins charitable vehicles, reinforcing the financing pathway. *(Docs: EFTAO0027019, EFTAO00587879, EFTAO00080250)*
- **Structure chart**: LDB owns Dionysus -> Odysseus -> Jet Aviation, which dry-leases the aircraft to Elysium/Apollo, keeping the plane outside the subsidiaries listed in Apollos Exhibit 21.1 (20222025). *(Doc: EFTAO00606411; earlier Exhibit 21.1 files.)*

## 2. Operations & Manifest Evidence
- **Flight manifests (20182019)**: every Plan D manifest for N212JE (Trips 31, 38, 80, 93, 136, etc.) lists Jeffrey Epstein as Passenger #1, details flights between St. Thomas, Teterboro, West Palm Beach, Paris, and other destinations, and is stamped CONFIDENTIAL / SUBJECT TO PROTECTIVE ORDER. Each page shows the flight desk as Plan D c/o Atlantic / attn: Larry Visoski (West Palm Beach). *(Docs: EFTAO0133526, EFTAO0133529, EFTAO01267394/87, HOUSE_OVERSIGHT_005290, etc.)*
- **Visoski coordination**: Feb 3, 2019 email explains that if Trump remains on the PBI tarmac, Plan D would divert to Boca Atlantic, demonstrating strategic routing around presidential travel. *(Doc: EFTAO02277042)*
- **Passenger communication**: Raafat Al-Sabagh iMessages (Oct 2016) show him coordinating travel with Epsteins network (“Still in Paris?” / “Just arrived in London”). *(Doc: EFTAO01614140)*
- **FAA confirmation**: 2021 discovery correspondence reaffirms Visoski as manager, Epstein as member, and details the tail-number change and Georgia hangar sighting. *(Doc: EFTAO0023439)*
